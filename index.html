I am a recent PhD graduate from the School of Computer Science at the University of Oklahoma, advised by Dr. Chris Weaver. My core research interests lie at the intersection of human-computer interaction, coordination, and information visualization. I focus on developing user interactions and architecting data pipelines to support interactive exploration of hierarchical data structures. More broadly, I am passionate about advancing the frontiers of machine learning, artificial intelligence, and deep learning, especially in how these technologies can be harnessed to enhance data-driven decision-making and user experience. I am also increasingly interested in the applications and design of generative AI systems, including multimodal interfaces that integrate visualization, language, and model-driven insights for more effective human-AI collaboration.
